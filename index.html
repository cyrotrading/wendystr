How it works
Fee wallet triggers

A Helius webhook monitors the fee wallet. When the balance crosses the threshold, the pipeline fires automatically — no human involvement, no manual approvals.

Convert & off-ramp

SOL fees swap to USDC via Jupiter. Bridge.xyz off-ramps USDC to USD and deposits into the linked brokerage account. Fully automated, no manual steps.

Buy & hold forever

Alpaca API executes a market buy of $WEN stock. Shares are held indefinitely — never sold. Every purchase adds to the float held off the open market, compounding pressure on short sellers.
